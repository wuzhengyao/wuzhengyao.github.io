Research of Zhengyao Wu

Research interests

Broadly, I'm interested in algebra, algebraic geometry, number theory, algebraic groups.

Specifically, I'm very interested in:

  • Algebra:
    • Field theory: working over local or global or function fields, Galois theory, Galois Cohomology...
    • Commutative algebra: Brauer groups, ramification problems, regular local rings, lozalizations and completions...
    • Associative algebra: finite dimensional division algebras, central simple algebras, maximal orders, involutions, valuations over non-commutative rings...
    • K-theory: Milnor K-theory, projective and free modules...
  • Algebraic geometry: resolution of singularities, rational points, projective homogeneous spaces...
  • Number theory: Quadratic forms over fields, Hermitian forms over rings, Hasse principles, Witt groups...
  • Algebraic groups: Semisimple groups of classical types, linear algebraic groups over special fields, Galois cohomology of linear algebraic groups...
I'm also mildly interested in Jordan algebra and related structures, exceptional semisimple groups, Chow groups, algebraic curves and surfaces, algebraic K-theory, etc.
