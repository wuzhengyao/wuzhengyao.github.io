Research interests

Broadly, I'm interested in algebra, number theory, algebraic groups, algebraic geometry and their interactions.

Specifically, I study various problems in quadratic and hermitian forms and central simple algebras (CSA): local-global principles, Springer's problem for odd degree extensions, $u$-invariants, period-index problems, ramification and valuation theory of CSAs, etc. Additionally, I study structures associated to forms or CSAs such as Witt groups, Brauer groups and semisimple linear algebraic groups of classical types. For connected linear algebraic groups, I concern local-global principles of their principal homogeneous spaces (i.e. torsors) and projective homogeneous spaces (i.e. twisted flag varieties). My proofs implement tools such as Galois cohomology, cohomological invariants and patching.

For details please see my research statement.
